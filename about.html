About Me

Hello! I'm Zakaria Yahya, a passionate photographer who loves documenting life's moments through my lens. My photography journey began as a simple hobby, but over time it evolved into an artistic passion that seeks to highlight the beauty of small details and unique moments.

I focus on nature photography, urban life, and documentary photography. Through my images, I try to tell stories and present new angles of the world around us, aiming to convey a sensory experience that makes the viewer live the moment.

My Photography Journey

2024 - Present

Continuing travel and photography with a deeper focus, I transitioned to using a camera after shooting with a mobile phone. I visited places like Grace Village and Al-Arish, capturing the edges of Sinai. I also participated in the "Ibda'a" competition and exhibited my work at the University of Tanta.

2023

I began learning photography more seriously through workshops, photo tours, and traveling to places like Cairo, Al-Darb Al-Ahmar, and Fuji.

2022

I discovered my love for photography and started exploring it as a hobby, initially using a mobile phone, and began developing my skills and vision.
