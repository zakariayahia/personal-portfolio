Arish

"In the heart of Sinai lies Al-Arish — a city of golden sands, turquoise sea, and a proud Bedouin spirit. Through my lens, I captured the thrill of camel racing, the cultural heartbeat of the desert, and the warmth of traditions passed down through generations. This project celebrates the vibrant soul of Sinai and the stories etched in every step of the race."
