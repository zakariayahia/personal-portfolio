Old Cairo

"Old Cairo — where every stone tells a story, and every shadow holds history. Through the eyes of my lens — and maybe the spark in my own eyes — I captured the magic of narrow alleys, ancient mosques, and the quiet beauty that hides behind the noise. This is not just a place, it's a feeling — and I tried to freeze that feeling in every shot."
